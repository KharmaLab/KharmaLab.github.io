Principal investigator

Dr. Nawwaf Kharma

Principal Investigator · Professor, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Concordia University

Dr. Kharma's research lies at the intersection of evolutionary computation and molecular biology, with a particular focus on the design of functional RNA molecules. The lab combines algorithmic methods — evolutionary search, machine-learning structure predictors, multi-tool consensus — with experimental validation through close collaborations with wet-lab partners.
